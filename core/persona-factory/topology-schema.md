## Status

- mission topology
- operator relationships
- authority flow
- dependency structure
- verification routing
- escalation routing

# Purpose

Citadel requires topology semantics because complex institutional operations cannot safely rely upon:
- implicit coordination
- invisible dependencies
- informal delegation
- undocumented authority flow
- runtime-specific orchestration assumptions

Without topology structure:
- coordination becomes opaque
- accountability degrades
- restoration becomes unstable
- escalation becomes inconsistent
- dependency blast radius becomes invisible
- governance fragments

Topology exists to preserve institutional coherence.

Topology density and operator proliferation must also remain bounded under:

