# Minimal Survivable Profile

## Status

Canonical AFW operational profile.

This document defines the smallest governance footprint capable of preserving:
- institutional legitimacy
- epistemic integrity
- bounded authority
- operational coherence
- audit survivability
- restoration viability

under constrained operational conditions.

The Minimal Survivable Profile exists to preserve survivable institutional cognition without governance maximalism.

---

# Purpose

AFW recognizes that not all missions can sustain:
- maximal audit depth
- dense topology
- full arbitration layering
- extensive coordination overhead
- exhaustive restoration semantics
- high-density verification

Without bounded operational profiles:
- governance becomes unaffordable
- operators become overloaded
- coordination becomes unsustainable
- institutional adoption collapses

The Minimal Survivable Profile exists to preserve:

```txt
minimum legitimate institutional operation
```

under survivability constraints.

---

# Core Principle

Minimal governance is not governance abandonment.

The profile exists to preserve:
- institutional coherence
- survivability
- epistemic discipline
- restoration continuity
- bounded legitimacy

with the smallest sustainable governance footprint.

The objective is:

```txt
minimum survivable institutional cognition
```

not:
- maximal sophistication
- maximal procedural density
- maximal audit recursion
